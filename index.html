    Frequently Asked Questions

    Will the vehicle get repaired on the spot?

    Yes, the vehicle will be repaired on the spot as we have special vehicles deployed for this purpose.

    How much will it cost?

    The cost of repair depends upon the type of repair that has to be performed on the vehicle.

    How long does it take for the puncture repair?

    Usually it takes 20-30 minutes on an average.

    Is there any discount or offers available ?

    First repair is free. For further discounts, you have to take subscription to be a prime member and then additional discounts would be provided.

    What are the modes of payment for repair?

    The vehicle that would reach for repair will have a QR Code and other details through which you can pay like UPI, Debit Cards, Credit Cards, Wallets, etc.

    What is the mode of payment for Subscription?

    You can avail subscription by paying through UPI and Net-banking only.
